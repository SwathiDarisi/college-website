Departments

Our college offers a variety of departments to cater to different fields of engineering and technology. Each department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members to provide students with a comprehensive education.

  • Computer Science & Engineering Focuses on software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science.
  • Mechanical Engineering: Covers design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and robotics.
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering: Involves the study of power systems, control systems, and communications.
  • Civil Engineering: Specializes in structural design, transportation, and environmental engineering.
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA): Advanced studies in computer applications, software development, and IT management.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): Comprehensive business management education with specializations in finance, marketing, and human resources.

Programs Offered

We offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to provide students with a comprehensive education in their chosen fields. Our programs are designed to combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech): A four-year undergraduate program offering specializations in various engineering disciplines.
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech): A two-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced technical knowledge and research in specific engineering fields.
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA): A three-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced studies in computer applications and software development.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA): A two-year postgraduate program offering comprehensive business management education with specializations in finance, marketing, and human resources.

Eligibility Criteria for Admission

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility:

  • Must have completed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) as core subjects.
  • A minimum aggregate of 50% marks (may vary based on category and college policies).
  • Admission through national/state-level entrance exams (such as JEE Main or other recognized entrance tests).

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor’s degree (BCA/B.Sc. (IT/CS)/B.Tech or any equivalent degree) with Mathematics as a subject at 10+2 or graduation level.
  • A minimum aggregate of 50% marks in the qualifying degree (may vary).
  • Admission through national/state-level entrance exams (such as NIMCET or other recognized entrance tests).

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
  • A minimum aggregate of 50% marks in graduation (may vary based on category and institution norms).
  • Admission through national/state-level entrance exams (such as CAT, MAT, XAT, or institution-specific entrance tests).
